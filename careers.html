Careers at The Social Market Collective

We're always looking for organized, positive, and community-minded individuals who love supporting small businesses and helping bring events to life. If you thrive in fast-paced environments and enjoy working with local entrepreneurs, we would love to hear from you.

Event Support Team Member

Part-Time / Event-Based  ·  Chicagoland Area

The Social Market Collective is seeking dependable and enthusiastic Event Support Team Members to assist with the execution of our artisan markets, pop-up events, and community showcases. This role is perfect for someone who enjoys working with people, staying organized, and being part of a high-energy event environment.

Responsibilities

  • Assist with vendor check-in and registration upon arrival
  • Help direct vendors to their assigned booth spaces
  • Support table and layout setup according to the event floor plan
  • Assist with event signage placement and check-in station setup
  • Help answer vendor and guest questions throughout the event
  • Ensure vendor spaces remain organized and compliant with event guidelines
  • Support crowd flow and general guest assistance
  • Assist with event breakdown and cleanup at the end of the event
  • Communicate with the event coordinator regarding any issues or needs

Qualifications

  • Friendly and professional communication skills
  • Strong organizational skills and attention to detail
  • Ability to work in a fast-paced event environment
  • Ability to lift up to 30 lbs (tables, signage, supplies)
  • Reliable transportation to event locations
  • Weekend availability

Ideal Candidate

This position is ideal for someone who enjoys being part of the local small business community and wants hands-on experience working behind the scenes at large community events.

Personal Assistant

Part-Time  ·  Hybrid (Remote + Event Days)

The Social Market Collective and Beaded For You Co. are looking for a highly organized Personal Assistant to help support the day-to-day operations of both brands. This role requires someone who is detail-oriented, proactive, and comfortable managing multiple tasks in a fast-paced entrepreneurial environment.

Responsibilities

  • Assist with email management and vendor communication
  • Help manage event applications and vendor approvals
  • Coordinate vendor invoices and payment tracking
  • Assist with event planning logistics and vendor organization
  • Help schedule and manage social media content and promotions
  • Assist with event marketing and outreach to potential vendors
  • Support preparation for upcoming markets and pop-up events
  • Help organize event materials and supplies
  • Assist with administrative tasks for Beaded For You Co. and Social Market Collective
  • Provide day-of event assistance when needed

Qualifications

  • Highly organized with strong attention to detail
  • Strong written communication skills
  • Comfortable using Google Docs, email, and social media platforms
  • Ability to manage multiple projects and deadlines
  • Self-motivated and proactive problem solver
  • Interest in small business, events, or marketing is a plus

Ideal Candidate

This position is ideal for someone who enjoys working closely with a growing small business and wants hands-on experience in event planning, marketing, and small business operations.
