About

The Social Market Collective Events was founded in November 2025 by entrepreneur and small business owner Caelin Porter, the creator behind the handmade jewelry brand Beaded For You Co.

After participating in countless markets as a vendor herself, Caelin experienced firsthand both the excitement and the challenges that can come with local markets — from inconsistent foot traffic to events that lacked intentional planning for vendor success. Through those experiences, she saw an opportunity to build something better.

The Social Market Collective was created with a simple but powerful mission: to design thoughtfully curated events that genuinely support local small businesses while bringing communities together.

Each event is carefully planned to create an engaging shopping experience for guests while ensuring vendors receive the visibility, organization, and foot traffic they deserve. From artisan markets and themed shopping events to community pop-ups and boutique showcases, every event is built around connection, creativity, and collaboration.

In addition to hosting markets, The Social Market Collective also offers event consulting and planning services for local businesses looking to host grand openings, customer appreciation events, workshops, boutique events, and brand launches. With a strong background in marketing and event production, Caelin helps businesses create meaningful experiences that drive foot traffic, build community, and elevate their brand.

Today, The Social Market Collective partners with local venues, artisans, and small business owners across the Chicagoland area to host vibrant markets that celebrate entrepreneurship and strengthen the local business community.

At its heart, The Social Market Collective is more than just an events company — it's a platform dedicated to helping small businesses grow.
